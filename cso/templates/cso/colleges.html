CPSalsa – California Polytechnic State University
San Luis Obispo, CA

CPSalsa has been at Cal Poly since 1920 hosting salsa nights every Friday – even through the summer! CPSalsa is also responsible for hosting many events throughout the year including SLOSX, a salsa exchange held for an entire weekend in San Luis Obispo.
