Ticket Sales

CSO ticket sales are online only, no door sales. We will be selling competition and performance spectator tickets at the door for friends and family, but if you plan to attend all night for social dancing you should purchase your ticket online.

Tickets are non-transferable but we offer free no-questions-asked refunds until April 22nd. If you need to refund your ticket just go back to the registration page and complete your refund online.
