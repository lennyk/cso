Event Information

Tickets

Full tickets including workshops, performances, competition, and social dancing are $28. Full tickets are only available online.

Full tickets are non-transferable but we offer free no-questions-asked refunds until April 18th. If you need to refund your ticket just go back to the registration page and complete your refund online.

Spectator tickets, allowing friends and family to attend the performances and competition are $8. Spectator tickets will only be available at the event, cash only. If you plan to attend workshops and the all-night social dancing you must purchase a regular ticket online. Spectators will only be allowed to attend the event from 8:30pm - 10:00pm. Children 6 and under can may enter for free.

The Competition

The rules and structure of the competition have been decided collaboratively by all the colleges with input and voting from each school.
