The Collegiate Salsa Open is the premiere salsa summit where dancers from colleges and communities all over California will come together to celebrate our unique salsa scene through competitions, performances, and all-night social dancing. The CSO is organized by the college dance community for the college dance community.

The first annual CSO will be held on April 25th, 2015.
