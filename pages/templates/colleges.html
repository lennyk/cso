University of Southern California – Break On 2 Los Angeles, CA

In the house Performing Competing

Break On 2 is a Salsa performance team and a social dancing club at USC. Break On 2 puts on an annual dance showcase featuring many dance styles and special guests. They host monthly socials for the USC community and the general public at the USC campus.

California Polytechnic State University – CPSalsa San Luis Obispo, CA

In the house

CPSalsa has been at Cal Poly since 1920 hosting salsa nights every Friday – even through the summer! CPSalsa is also responsible for hosting many events throughout the year including SLOSX, a salsa exchange held for an entire weekend in San Luis Obispo.
